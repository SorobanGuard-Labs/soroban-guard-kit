# Audit readiness checklist

- [ ] Threat model documents actors, assets, and trust boundaries.
- [ ] Privileged entrypoints list expected callers.
- [ ] Storage keys, TTL assumptions, and migrations are documented.
- [ ] Positive, negative, boundary, and regression tests are identified.
- [ ] Deployment, rollback, and incident notes are available.
- [ ] Known issues and unresolved assumptions are listed.
